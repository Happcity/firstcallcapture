Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about First Call Capture

Getting Started

How does First Call Capture work?

When someone calls your business and you can't answer, First Call Capture automatically sends them a branded SMS text message within 10 seconds. You also receive an instant notification with the caller's number and a one-tap callback button.

How long does setup take?

Setup takes less than 5 minutes! Simply sign up, verify your email, choose your plan, and we'll automatically provision a dedicated phone number for your business. You can customize your messages immediately from your dashboard.

Do I need to change my existing phone number?

No! We provide you with a new dedicated phone number specifically for First Call Capture. You can use this as your main business line or forward calls from your existing number to it.

Can I customize the messages?

Absolutely! You can fully customize both your voice greeting and SMS auto-reply message from your dashboard. Include your business name, website link, and any custom text you want.

Pricing & Billing

When will I be charged?

You won't be charged until your 14-day free trial ends. No credit card required to start the trial. You can cancel anytime during the trial at no cost.

Can I change plans later?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from your dashboard. Changes take effect immediately, and billing is prorated automatically.

What happens after the trial?

If you love the service, your chosen plan will begin and your card will be charged monthly. If not, simply cancel before the trial ends and you won't be charged anything.

Do you offer refunds?

Yes! We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied with First Call Capture for any reason, contact us within 30 days of your first payment for a full refund.

Are there any hidden fees?

No hidden fees! Your monthly subscription includes your phone number, unlimited SMS during our launch promotion, and all core features. Add-ons are clearly priced and completely optional.

Features & Functionality

What's included in the Starter plan?

The Starter plan includes 1 dedicated phone number, up to 150 calls/month (unlimited during launch), unlimited auto-reply SMS, custom voice and SMS messages, one-tap callback, and dashboard management.

How quickly are texts sent?

Texts are sent automatically within 10 seconds of a missed call. This immediate response keeps your leads warm and shows professionalism.

Can I add multiple phone numbers?

Each subscription includes 1 phone number. If you need multiple numbers (for different locations or departments), simply add additional subscriptions through your dashboard.

Does it work with voicemail?

Yes! First Call Capture works alongside your voicemail. Even if callers leave a message, they'll still receive the instant SMS response, increasing your chances of converting them.

Can customers reply to the SMS?

Yes! Your auto-reply creates a two-way SMS conversation. When customers reply, you can respond directly from your phone or dashboard.

What analytics do you provide?

Professional and Business plans include call tracking, timestamp logs, phone number history, missed call reports, average response time tracking, and weekly email summaries.

What is the AI Voice Assistant?

The AI Voice Assistant ($199/mo) can actually answer calls like a human, take detailed messages, answer frequently asked questions, and transfer calls to you when needed. It handles up to 100 calls per month.

Our CRM integration ($49/mo per CRM) works with HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive, and other major platforms. Automatically create contacts, sync call history, and keep your pipeline updated.
