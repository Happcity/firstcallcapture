Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about First Call Capture

Getting Started

How does First Call Capture work?

When someone calls your business phone and you can't answer, First Call Capture automatically sends them a personalized text message within seconds. The message lets them know you received their call and will get back to them soon. You can customize this message to match your brand and business needs.

How long does setup take?

Setup takes less than 5 minutes! Simply sign up, choose your plan, and we'll provide you with a dedicated phone number. Forward your missed calls to this number (we'll show you how), customize your auto-reply message, and you're ready to go. No technical knowledge required.

Do I need to change my business phone number?

No! You keep your existing business phone number. We provide you with a dedicated forwarding number that you'll use to forward only your missed calls. Your customers will never see or interact with this forwarding number - they only see your regular business number.

Can I customize the auto-reply message?

Absolutely! You have complete control over your auto-reply message. Include your business name, hours of operation, website link, or any other information you want. You can also include your callback number so customers can easily call you back with one tap. Update your message anytime through your dashboard.

Yes! The 14-day trial is completely free with no credit card required upfront. You'll get full access to all features during the trial period. If you decide First Call Capture isn't right for you, simply don't subscribe and you won't be charged anything.

What's your refund policy?

Features & Functionality

What features are included in the Starter plan?

The Starter plan includes: 1 dedicated phone number, unlimited auto-reply SMS (during launch promotion), dashboard management, call count tracking, messages sent tracking, SMS notifications to you when someone calls, customizable SMS and voice messages, one-tap callback links, and website links in SMS.

How fast are the text messages sent?

Text messages are sent within 2-5 seconds after a missed call. Our system is built on Twilio's industry-leading infrastructure to ensure reliable, fast delivery. The exact timing may vary slightly based on the caller's mobile carrier.

Can I use multiple phone numbers?

Each subscription plan includes one dedicated phone number. If you need multiple numbers (for different locations, departments, or businesses), you can subscribe to additional plans. Each plan operates independently with its own settings and analytics.

What happens if someone leaves a voicemail?

If a caller leaves a voicemail on your regular business line before the call forwards to First Call Capture, they'll still receive the auto-reply text. This ensures every caller gets acknowledgment, whether they leave a voicemail or not.

Can callers reply to the auto-text?

Yes! Callers can reply to the text message. You'll receive an SMS notification with their response, and you can continue the conversation via text or call them back directly. This creates a seamless two-way communication channel with your customers.

What analytics and reporting do you provide?

All plans include basic analytics like call count and messages sent. Professional and Business plans add call timestamps, phone number history, missed call reports, average response time tracking, and weekly email summaries. Business plans also include caller location tracking and monthly performance reviews.
